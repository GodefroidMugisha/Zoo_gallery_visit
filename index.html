We are happy to have you here, and we really appreciate you for being on the right website. With the help of RDB (Rwanda Development Board) that has national parks on its objectives, we introduced GODEFROID PARK where you can visit different animals and feel close to the creator as you see them. GUIDANCE

Visit us and feel the beauty of nature. We are announcing to every visitor to not leave the team support, however you are crossing over the park don't walk alone to avoid any trouble. If you feel uncomfortable, or feeling ill, please tell one of your guides to get quick support. If you find out that someone is missing, try to give information on time to see if we can help. Some of the animals we have, have their habits and we have the main way to act in front of them, please don't do any sign if you are not told to do so. Respect the rules and regulations so that you feel the beauty of nature with GODEFROID PARK.
