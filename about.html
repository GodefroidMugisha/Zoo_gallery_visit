How it started!!


My name is MUGISHA GODEFROID, two years ago i planned to visit Akagera National park and it took me two hours traveling there. I was tired after that long journey, I was suppossed to have fun with my friends but i was truly tired. That's how i got an idea of building GODEFROID PARK to support people with near by city park where everybody can visit at any time and have fun with friends without tiredness. From that day i asked for appointment with RDB(Rwanda Development Board) chief exective, to talk about my plan and he ended up telling me that i'm in the right way. to day, we host foreigners and international people from all the location over the world. that's why we keep telling you to "feel the beauty of nature with us".
